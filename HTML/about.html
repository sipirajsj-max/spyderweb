About Us

Sri Lanka's Premier Local & Digital Service Hub

Our Founder's Vision

I'm **Sipiraj**, a Software Engineer currently on a professional journey, and also a passionate writer. I recognized that while Sri Lanka has an abundant supply of local talent and opportunities, there was a missing link – a reliable medium to connect skilled workers with those who need them. I developed Spyder Web specifically so that communication gaps and barriers would no longer hinder Sri Lanka's creative and skill-based potential.

Our Purpose

Spyder Web is a dual-purpose platform. Firstly, it serves as a crucial medium for connecting **local skilled workers** (like Masons, Carpenters, Plumbers, and other manual laborers) directly with clients requiring local work. Secondly, this creation is designed to facilitate and streamline digital tasks, such as online data entry and typing jobs, offering opportunities for smaller online projects.

Why Choose Spyder Web?

By registering on this website, both local job seekers (experienced Masons, Carpenters, Plumbers) and clients can connect seamlessly across districts. Clients can easily identify and contact verified, experienced workers to ensure their projects are completed efficiently, cost-effectively, and with guaranteed quality, all without unnecessary hassle.
